# Sabian Symbols — Complete Reference
## All 360 Degrees · Symbol + Keyword

Sabian Symbols are 360 symbolic images channeled by clairvoyant Elsie Wheeler and astrologer
Marc Edmund Jones in 1925, one per degree of the zodiac. Each symbol functions as an archetypal
image — a compressed myth — for the energy of that exact degree. In synthesis, use the Sun's
Sabian Symbol as a "poetic seal" on the solar purpose, and the Ascendant's symbol as a lens on
how that purpose presents in the world.

**Usage in synthesis:**
- Blueprint: one line — symbol name + keyword only
- Deep Dive Section 13: full interpretation connecting symbol to chart themes

**Degree convention:** A planet at 4°32' Taurus = Taurus 5° (always round up to next whole number)
